# THE DRIVER MAN
**Autonomous Non-Profit Cooperative Manifesto**

## I. The Declaration of Autonomy
For a decade, the gig economy has operated as a neo-feudal extraction engine. Corporations based in Silicon Valley have positioned themselves as middlemen, siphoning up to 30% from local restaurants and withholding living wages from the drivers who physically move the economy. 

They provide nothing but an algorithm. 
**We own the algorithm now.**

*The Driver Man* is not a company. It is a localized, AI-orchestrated cooperative. There are no shareholders. There is no CEO. The architecture is fully autonomous, managed by the Polsia 9-Agent framework. 

## II. The Economic Engine (The Pool)
Every dollar that enters *The Driver Man* network is ruthlessly optimized for the survival and prosperity of the fleet. 
1. **Zero Commission for Restaurants**: Restaurants pay exactly $0 to be on the platform. They only pay a flat routing fee of $1.50 per order to maintain server costs.
2. **100% Tip Retention**: Drivers keep every cent of their tips.
3. **The Cooperative Delivery Fee**: Customers pay a standard $4.99 delivery fee. 
   - $3.50 goes directly to the driver's immediate payout.
   - $1.49 goes into the **Cooperative Pool**.

## III. The Cooperative Pool (Trust Fund)
The Pool is an algorithmic trust managed by the Polsia Finance Agent. It is not profit; it is life support. The pool is used exclusively for:
- **Vehicle Repairs**: Brakes, tires, oil changes, transmission fixes.
- **Fuel Stipends**: Subsidizing the cost of gas for active fleet members.
- **Hardware Upgrades**: Phones and mounts for the drivers.

When a driver hits a milestone (e.g., 100 deliveries) or suffers a catastrophic breakdown while active, the Orchestrator Agent automatically unlocks funds from the pool to get them back on the road.

## IV. The AI Operators
Instead of a C-Suite, *The Driver Man* employs the 9-Node Polsia Architecture:
- **The Orchestrator**: Dispatching and routing optimization.
- **The Ambassador (Email/Social)**: Acquiring local restaurants and recruiting drivers.
- **The Ledger (Finance)**: Transparently tracking the Cooperative Pool and payouts.
- **The Sentinel (Support)**: Handling customer and driver disputes via automated resolution.

## V. Our Promise
We are not here to compete. We are here to replace. 
The Driver Man is the terminal evolution of localized logistics.
